Designer and writer

Hi, I am Benjamin.

I first learned about web design when I was 12. What started as simple curiosity evolved over time into mild obsession throughout high school.

Over the last five years I've taken on occasional web projects, while focusing heavily on creative writing. Better Site brings that mix of design and writing together to offer an affordable, friendly service to small businesses all across New Zealand.

I look forward to hearing more about your business!

What working with me feels like

You get direct communication, fast support, and a clear design direction with no obligation to commit to a full build. I am available for calls if you want to talk through your business and/or current website and what needs to change.
