What affects the quote

The right website price depends on what the site has to do.

Page count matters, but so does the amount of writing, service detail, SEO content, forms, reviews, and post-launch support your business needs.

01

Number of Pages

A simple website may only need a homepage, services, about, and contact. A stronger search presence may need separate service pages.

02

Copy and Content

Some businesses already have clear wording and photos. Others need more help turning services, proof, and FAQs into useful website content.

03

Care After Launch

Hosting, backups, edits, and SEO content updates can be handled through a care plan if you want the website looked after over time.
