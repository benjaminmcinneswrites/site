How the free mockup works.

The process is designed to remove pressure. You get to see a real homepage direction before deciding whether a full website makes sense.

1

Tell me about your business

Short form with your business details, goals, current website, and preferred style.

2

I review your business

I look at your current online presence, competitors, services, and what customers need to see.

3

You receive a free homepage mockup

Within 5 business days, you get a clear preview of what your new homepage could look like.

4

You decide what happens next

If you like it, we can turn it into a full website. If not, no pressure.
