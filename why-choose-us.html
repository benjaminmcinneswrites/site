Why choose Better Site

More thoughtful than a website builder. Simpler than a big agency.

Using a website builder can feel like the quickest way to get online, while a big agency can feel like the safest way to get something polished.

For many small businesses, the better option sits in the middle: brand-focused strategy, thoughtful design, and direct communication without the agency price tag or endless back-and-forth nightmares.

What matters most

Five reasons businesses choose Better Site.

01

A Clearer First Impression

Your website should instantly show people that your business is real and worth contacting. I focus on making sites that feel branded rather than generic, with carefully planned copywriting and visual decisions that match the business you are building.

02

A Site That Converts Clicks to Sales

In order to convert well, visitors must know exactly where to look and where to click within the first five seconds. I think carefully about structure, headings, buttons, page flow, etc., so the site feels natural to navigate, especially for people viewing it on their phone.

03

Better Foundations for Being Found

A well SEO-optimized website needs clear page titles, useful headings, well-researched keywords, fast loading, and a specific structure that search engines favor. I build with these basics in mind so your site has a strong starting point in search engine results.

Made to fit

Made Around Your Business, Not Squeezed Into a Template

DIY builders can be useful, but they often lead to websites that feel as if they could belong to anyone. Every business is different, and every business has a brand: specific services or products, customer base, and reasons people choose you.

I bring design and writing together to build you a site that feels specific, human-authored, and aligned with what it is you actually offer.

04

Support After Launch

I understand that brands often want to make content changes to their site over time. I offer a few different hosting and care packages, starting as low as $19 a month, so your website stays online 24/7 and never becomes another thing you're left to manage alone.

05

A Simpler Way to Get a Professional Website

You don't need to deal with the overpriced, back-and-forth communication nightmare of big agencies. My service is practical for New Zealand small businesses who prioritize clear design, thoughtful wording, and ease of use.
