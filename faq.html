FAQ

Common questions before you request a mockup.

Short answers, no sales fog. The free homepage mockup is meant to make the next decision easier.

Is the homepage mockup really free?

Yes. I will create a free homepage mockup so you can see what your business could look like online before deciding whether to move forward.

Is it a full free website?

No. It is a free homepage mockup, not a complete website. It gives you a clear preview without requiring a full build upfront.

How long does it take?

The free homepage mockup is delivered within 5 business days. Full website timelines depend on the size of the project.

Do you only work with Christchurch businesses?

No. I work with businesses across New Zealand.

Can I just get hosting or a care plan?

Yes. You do not need a brand new website to ask for help. If you already have a site and only want hosting, ongoing care, support, content edits, or SEO content updates, use the contact form with your current website link and I will get back to you with a quote.

What if I already have a website?

That is fine. I can either create a mockup showing how your current site could be improved, or review the site for hosting, care plan, and content update options.

Do I have to commit after receiving the mockup?

No. There is no pressure and no obligation.

Can we have a call?

Yes. I am available for calls, especially if you want to explain the business, the current website, or what you want the site to help with.

Still deciding?

The free mockup is designed for that.

Request a homepage preview and see whether the direction feels right before committing to anything.
